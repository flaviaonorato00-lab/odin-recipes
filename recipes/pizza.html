Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe is made with simple ingredients that are easy to find. The dough is prepared at home, left to rest, and then shaped by hand. It is topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and a little olive oil before being baked in a very hot oven.

After baking, fresh basil is added to give the pizza a fresh and aromatic flavor. The result is a homemade pizza with a soft crust, melted cheese, and a classic taste that makes it perfect for sharing at home.

Ingredients

  • 300 g flour
  • 180 ml water
  • 7 g dry yeast
  • 6 g salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 200 g tomato sauce
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves (to taste)

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the water and olive oil, then mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for 1 to 2 hours.
  5. Preheat the oven to the highest temperature.
  6. Shape the dough into a round pizza base.
  7. Spread the tomato sauce on the dough.
  8. Add the mozzarella and other toppings.
  9. Bake the pizza until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  10. Add fresh basil, slice, and serve.
