Tomato Pasta

Description

This tomato pasta recipe is a simple and flavorful dish that can be prepared quickly. It features a rich tomato sauce made from fresh tomatoes, garlic, and herbs, served over al dente pasta. This dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends.

The combination of the tangy tomato sauce and the tender pasta creates a comforting meal that is both satisfying and delicious. Garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese, this tomato pasta is sure to become a favorite in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 400 g fresh tomatoes or canned tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the fresh or canned tomatoes to the pan. Cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  4. Season the sauce with dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the tomato sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
